About Our App

Our "RadioLens AI" app leverages cutting-edge LLM to automate the generation of radiology reports from medical images such as X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans. This compact and efficient model integrates both vision and language capabilities to understand and describe medical images with high accuracy, helping radiologists save time and effort while ensuring consistent reporting.
